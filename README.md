📊 Predictive Sales Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Predictive Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, customer returns, discount strategies, and product performance across multiple locations and sales platforms.

The dashboard enables businesses to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), evaluate product profitability, identify high-performing markets, and make informed, data-driven decisions to improve sales performance and customer satisfaction.

🎯 Business Problem

Organizations selling products across multiple regions and online platforms often face challenges in:

Tracking overall sales performance
Identifying top-performing products
Monitoring customer return rates
Evaluating discount effectiveness
Comparing platform-wise sales
Understanding regional demand
Improving profitability

This dashboard addresses these challenges through interactive visualizations and KPI monitoring.

📌 Key Insights

💰 Revenue Performance
The business generated 22.91 Million in total revenue by selling 658K units, indicating a strong sales volume across all product categories.
Despite offering an average discount of 12.44%, the business maintained a Net Revenue of 1.29 Million, demonstrating that discount strategies continue to support revenue generation.

🛍 Product Category Analysis
Vitamin and Mineral categories are the highest revenue-generating product segments, each contributing approximately 4.3 Million.
Performance and Protein categories are the next strongest contributors, generating nearly 2.9 Million each.
Categories including Hydration, Herbal, and Sleep Aid generate comparatively lower revenue, suggesting opportunities for targeted promotions or portfolio optimization.

📈 Monthly Sales Trend
Monthly revenue fluctuates throughout the year.
March records the highest revenue at approximately 2.27 Million, indicating peak customer demand.
Revenue experiences a significant decline in April, falling to approximately 1.69 Million, before gradually stabilizing during the remaining months.
The observed fluctuations suggest seasonal purchasing behavior and indicate opportunities for demand forecasting and targeted marketing campaigns.

🌍 Regional Performance
Revenue distribution indicates that the United Kingdom (UK) is the highest-performing market.
Canada contributes the second-highest revenue.
United States (USA) follows closely, indicating relatively balanced sales across major operating regions.

🛒 Platform Performance
Walmart generates the highest revenue among all platforms.
iHerb performs almost equally well.
Amazon contributes comparatively lower revenue and may require improved promotional strategies or marketplace optimization.

🔄 Product Return Analysis
Overall return percentage remains very low at 1.02%, indicating high customer satisfaction and product acceptance.
Vitamin C records the highest product return percentage (approximately 1.12%).
Most products maintain return rates close to 1%, suggesting consistent product quality across the catalog.
